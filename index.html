What Is It?

A Ceaser Cipher is a type of cipher where you have a key, which is an integer, and then shift the letter up by whatever your key is. For example, assume we have a key of 1. Applying this key, a becomes b, b becomes c, c becomes d, and so on.

How To Use?

1) Choose to either encrypt or decrypt by clicking your desired option
2) Type your text in the box under the area where you selected your option
3) Click the button under the input area
4) Congrats! You now hve encrypted (or decrupted) text!
